Sentien Connect Privacy policy

Your data is your data.

Sentien Connect does not, and will never, collect your data.

  • Sentien Connect requires the Bluetooth and BluetoothLE permission to let you search through your devices and connect to a Sentien Audio device. Nothing is stored, nothing is sent or shared.
  • To use Bluetooth, Sentien Connect needs access to location permissions. App does not derive any physical location. No information is shared with Sentien nor any other party.
  • Only information Sentien Connect transmits are software bugs and crash reports that help us to improve the future version. Bug reports are sent via RollBar service and is compliant to their privacy policy.
