Our processing of your personal data requires a legal basis. We will not process your personal data unless we have a valid legal reason to do so. Therefore, we will only process your personal data if:

- we have obtained your prior consent;

In addition to the above, you will be notified that your personal information may be shared with third parties, and you will be able to choose not to share that information.

Transfers outside the European Economic Area: Whenever we transfer personal data to countries outside the European Economic Area, we will ensure that they are transferred in accordance with this Privacy Notice and the applicable data protection laws.

7. How is information about you secured with us?

We design our systems and equipment with your security and privacy in mind. Our security procedures mean that we may from time to time require proof of identity before disclosing personal information.
